Zuitt IT Solutions | About Us

We are a team of passionate and purpose-driven individuals, helping shape the future of IT Education in the Philippines

Zuitt, a globally recognized startup, is at the cutting-edge of IT education, offering a comprehensive online web development coding bootcamp locally and across the globe. Since its inception in 2016, Zuitt has been driven by a core mission: to empower individuals with the essential coding skills needed to secure excellent job prospects and propel their careers forward.

Our curriculum is designed to meet the needs of a diverse audience, including both IT majors and non-IT majors, as well as those seeking to enhance their skills for a career in web development or considering a career change.

Over the course of more than half a decade, Zuitt Coding Bootcamp has maintained its commitment to delivering world-class education, consistently nurturing top-tier web developers ready to excel in the tech industry. Our unwavering dedication to shaping the future of IT education has placed us at the helm of the global ed-tech scene.
